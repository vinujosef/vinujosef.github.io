User Experience Guidelines for Improving Quality of Involvement in Mobile Applications

In Progress | Expected : June 2017

Lot of organizations are going mobile only for their applications and by 2020 the number of mobile apps available are going to grow exponentially and outnumber web apps and desktop apps. Therefore there exists the need for building quality products that can be used by all stakeholders of a product. The paper addresses the guidelines that developers and designers need to take care of while creating mobile apps.

Improving Usability for E-Commerce Applications Using Eye Tracking Technology

In Progress | Expected : December 2017

E-commerce is a continuous blooming industry which is being adopted both by new and existing players in the business. There exists the need to find out if the mobile app developed is usable for the users. Within couple of years, mobile gadgets will have the hardware to detect and track eye movements. The paper addresses the use of eye tracking technology in finding and measuring usability of e-commerce apps and thereby suggesting methods to improve usability and user experience.
